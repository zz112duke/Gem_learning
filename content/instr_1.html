You will be navigating through different scenes and make choices. Your goal is to maximize your reward in each of the scenes.
Whenever you see a circle, press the H key.
Whenever you see a square, do not press any key.
You will have 800ms to respond to each shape.
A slow response will be considered as incorrect.
Now click on "Practice" to practice this part of the game.
Remember that you should respond as accurately as possible.
Also note that you will only be given feedback in this practice game but not in the real game.
