Sometimes, the background color of the screen will change from white to a different color, like this:

Whenever the background is green or blue, the goal of the game is different.
Now, instead of pressing the H key to every circle, you will press the X key or the C key to every circle.
Your job is to figure out when to press X and when to press C through trial and error. You will not see any squares in this part of the game.
To help you figure out when X is correct and when C is correct, you will see feedback after each press.

When you are trying to figure out when to press X and when to press C , pay attention to the backgroud color, and the stripes on each circle.
Your response will not be timed on these shapes with green or blue background so you can think about what key to press.
Now click on "Continue" to move on.
