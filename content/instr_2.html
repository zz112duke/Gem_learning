Your gemology training will take place in two different locations: a faraway mountain and a deserted road, like this:

When you are on the mountain, indicate whether the gem originated from the top of the mountain by pressing the Up arrow key or the bottom of the mountain by clicking the Down arrow key on the keyboard.
When you are on the road, indicate whether the gem originated from the left side of the road by pressing the Left arrow key or the right side of the road by clicking the Right arrow key on the keyboard.
